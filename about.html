About the Platform

Empowering Citizens.
Strengthening Democracy.

CivicPulse is a comprehensive civic engagement platform designed to bring transparency, accountability, and active participation to the heart of governance. We believe informed citizens build stronger communities.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between citizens and their government by providing easy access to candidate data, fund allocations, and a channel to report civic issues — all in one place.

Our Vision

A future where every citizen, regardless of location or background, has the tools and knowledge to actively participate in governance and hold their representatives accountable.

What CivicPulse Offers

01

Complaint Portal

Upload photos and geo-tagged reports of civic failures — potholes, sewage overflow, water logging, broken streetlights. Track the status of your complaint in real time.

02

Candidate Transparency

Research every candidate's education, declared assets, criminal background, and manifesto promises. Compare candidates side-by-side before you vote.

03

Fund Tracking Dashboard

Interactive charts show where your tax money goes. See sector-wise allocation, year-over-year comparisons, and spending transparency at a glance.

04

AI-Powered Chatbot

Ask questions about RTI filing, voting procedures, government policies, or your rights as a citizen. Powered by Google Gemini for accurate, instant answers.

05

Civic Learning Hub

Understand democracy, policies, and your constitutional rights through curated, easy-to-digest learning modules — perfect for first-time voters and seasoned citizens alike.

06

Community & Groups

Join neighborhood groups, add friends, invite others, and collaborate on civic campaigns. Strength in numbers — drive change as a collective.
